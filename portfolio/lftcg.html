Overview

LFTCG is a trading card marketplace concept developed with the goal of creating a Canadian-focused alternative within a space largely dominated by international platforms.

The project combined brand development, motion graphics, and e-commerce design to build a complete digital ecosystem tailored to collectors and players across Canada.

Objectives

The project aimed to address the lack of localized infrastructure for Canadian trading card communities, where existing marketplaces often overlooked regional needs.

The goal was to create a system that:
❖ Establishes a recognizable Canadian identity within the TCG space
❖ Supports a marketplace structure for buying and selling cards
❖ Builds a strong visual presence through branding and motion content

Process

The project began with the development of the brand itself, including naming, identity, and visual direction. The name “LFTCG” (Looking For TCG) was created to directly reflect the language and behavior of trading communities.

All visual assets were developed from the ground up, ensuring a consistent and original identity across the platform.

The platform was built using Shopify, with Webkul integrated to support marketplace functionality. This allowed the structure to move beyond a standard storefront into a multi-vendor system.

Motion graphics and visual content were also developed to support promotion and engagement, reinforcing the brand across digital channels.
