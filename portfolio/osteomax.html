Overview

A website designed for Osteomax, an osteopathy clinic looking to establish its first digital presence and improve accessibility for new patients.

The project focused on creating a clear, professional, and easy-to-navigate platform that reflects the clinic’s quality of care while making essential information readily available online.

Objectives

Osteomax had no existing website, limiting their visibility and making it difficult for potential patients to discover their services.

The goal was to create a system that:
❖ Communicates medical services clearly and accessibly
❖ Establishes trust through a clean and professional interface
❖ Improves online visibility and patient discovery

Process

The design approach focused on clarity and restraint, avoiding unnecessary visual complexity in favor of a structured and easy-to-understand layout.

A calm color palette, clear typography, and consistent spacing were used to guide users through the site while reinforcing a sense of professionalism and care.

Special attention was given to information architecture, ensuring that visitors could quickly understand who Osteomax is, what services are offered, and how to get in touch or book an appointment.

The final result is a clean and functional website that supports both credibility and usability, providing a strong foundation for the clinic’s online presence.
