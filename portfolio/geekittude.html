Trading Card Café & Community Space | 2021

Geekittude
Rebrand

A comprehensive brand identity redesign for a Montreal trading card shop focused on clarity, scalability, and community.

Role
Designer / Art Director
Tools
Adobe Suite, Figma, Shopify
Timeline
5 Weeks
Year
2021
Type
Professional

Overview

A full brand identity redesign for Geekittude, a Montreal-based trading card game and hobby shop. The project focused on modernizing the brand while preserving the community-driven identity that defined its presence.

Objectives

The original brand lacked consistency and scalability across platforms. With a long name and visually dense elements, the identity became difficult to apply clearly across signage, digital platforms, and merchandise.

The goal was to create a system that:
❖ Improves readability and recognition
❖ Scales across physical and digital environments
❖ Maintains the essence of Geekittude’s gaming culture and community

Process

The redesign focused on simplifying the visual language while strengthening structure and flexibility. A clean, tall logotype was developed to improve legibility and balance the length of the name. Supporting lines were introduced to subtly reference connection and community, inspired by the idea of linking players and spaces together.

The color system was refined into a bold blue palette, ensuring consistency while allowing strong contrast across applications. A standalone “G” icon was also created to function independently across signage, digital platforms, and smaller-scale assets. The system was then applied across multiple touchpoints, including storefront design, promotional materials, social media, and web presence.
