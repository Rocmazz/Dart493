Brand Identity | 2021

Geekittude
Rebrand

A comprehensive brand identity system redeveloped for a Trading Card Café & Community Space. Complete rebranding designing a modernized Geekittude while staying true to the community it was built on.

Role
Designer / Art Director
Tools
Adobe Suite, Figma, Shopify
Timeline
5 Weeks
Year
2021
Type
Professional

Overview

Objectives

Process

The project built on existing assets rather than replacing them. By reducing visual complexity and separating the wordmark from iconography, the new system brings clarity, consistency, and flexibility while staying true to the original brand.

The rebrand focused on simplifying Geekittude’s identity while preserving what made it recognizable. A tall, legible wordmark maintains the brand’s long name, while framing lines reference trading, movement, and community. A standalone “G” icon was introduced to function independently across digital and physical spaces, bridging Geekittude’s gaming and café identities.
