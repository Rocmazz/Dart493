I am a 3rd design student studying Design at Concordia University. My practice spans brand identity, print, digital, and motion design — united by a belief that strong concepts and sharp execution are non-negotiable.

I am drawn to work that communicates with confidence: design that knows exactly what it is and why it exists. Whether working on a full brand system or a single typographic poster, I bring the same level of intentionality and craft to every project.

Outside of studio practice, I am interested in Gaming, Film & Music which consistently inform the way I approach visual problems.
