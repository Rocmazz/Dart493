Overview

Streetrare is a streetwear brand concept developed in collaboration with a friend, combining pop culture references with a more fashion-driven visual identity.

The project explored how niche fandoms, character-inspired graphics, and contemporary streetwear aesthetics could be brought together into a brand with a distinct and recognizable voice.

Objectives

The intention behind Streetrare was to create a brand that felt both expressive and wearable, bridging the gap between fan culture and street fashion.

The goal was to create a system that:
❖ Builds a strong identity around pop culture and streetwear
❖ Appeals to audiences connected to gaming, anime, and geek culture
❖ Creates a visual foundation that can extend across apparel, graphics, and promotional content

Process

The project began by defining a visual direction that could balance energy, attitude, and recognizability without feeling overly literal or costume-like.

The branding process focused on capturing the boldness of streetwear while drawing inspiration from character-based and fan-centered references in a more stylized and wearable way.

As a collaborative project, the process involved developing ideas through exchange and iteration, shaping the brand’s tone, graphic language, and overall direction together.

The final result established a clear visual identity for Streetrare, creating a foundation that could support future apparel drops, collection concepts, and promotional materials.
